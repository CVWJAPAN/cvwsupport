A Global Call for Compassionate Technology: From Isolation to Empathy

A Proposal by Representative NOBUSHI (CVWJAPAN)

1. Personal Statement: A Voice from Silence

I was abandoned at three months old. Raised in an infant home, then a child welfare institution, I was never held by a parent, never heard the words "I'm here for you." Inside those walls, I suffered abuse. I was not just neglected—I was silenced.

Yet somehow, I survived. And now, I live not just to survive, but to ensure no child ever has to feel what I felt. I live with a rare disease. I live in poverty, sustained by a disability pension. And I live with a mission:

To create something the world has never seen—an AI-powered sanctuary for children that is born not from privilege, but from pain.

2. Why I Am Building This

Today's welfare systems miss what matters most: the invisible. The unheard. The children too afraid to speak, too hurt to ask.

Technology today can monitor, measure, predict—but few have asked: Can it care? Can it listen? Can it understand?

Through CVWJAPAN, I am developing a next-generation AI welfare platform that combines empathy, ethical design, and emotional insight. This is not surveillance. This is not automation. This is a digital companion—designed to feel, to wait, to whisper, “You’re not alone.”

3. The Vision: CVWCORE and CVW-REACT

  • CVWCORE: A secure, ethical AI infrastructure supporting child welfare, caregiver support, and protective communication.
  • CVW-REACT: An empathy-first AI that delivers nonjudgmental, situation-aware support. Not just detecting risk—but helping rebuild possibility.
  • Digital Children's Centers: Regional hubs blending safe physical space with AI, where children can engage, express, and explore safely.

This is not about systems. It's about souls.

4. What We Ask the World

We are seeking collaborators, funders, and allies who believe that dignity should define technology. We are not backed by governments. We are not corporate. We are an independent, nonprofit voice emerging from trauma, not theory.

To realize this vision, we are calling for international support to raise a minimum of 50,000,000 JPY (approx. $330,000 USD). Funds will be used to:

  • Develop and deploy our AI platform
  • Establish digital children’s centers
  • Enable multilingual, trauma-informed interaction systems
  • Maintain ethical, independent governance

You can help by:

  • Connecting us to funders and ethical AI developers
  • Offering translation, development, or advocacy support
  • Sharing this message with those who care
  • Make a donation via Square

5. Let This Be the Moment

Let this be the moment when a child raised in silence found their voice—and that voice was heard around the world. Let this be a new blueprint for human-first technology. Let this be the beginning of a future where no child is invisible.
